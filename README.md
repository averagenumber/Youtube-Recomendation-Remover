# YT Focus - an extension, made to help you stay away from youtube algorithms

Youtube is designed to keep your attention. YT Focus gives you back the control.
Instead of landing on a homepage full of recommendations, you`ll be redirected straight to your subscription feed - the content **you** actually signed up for.
What YT Focus does: 
* Redirects the Youtube  homepage to your subscriptions feed
* Disables access to the main page and Shorts
* Hides the recommended videos sidebar on watch pages

Every feature can be toggled individually. You decide what you want to limit and what to keep.
## Install
[Install from Firefox Add-ons](https://addons.mozilla.org/en-US/firefox/addon/yt-recommendation-remover/)
